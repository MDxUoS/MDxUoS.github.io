About me

My name is Fatma Alzaabi, a 22-year-old media enthusiast based in Dubai. I am currently in my final year at the University of Sharjah, majoring in Mass Communication with a concentration in Media Production.

Beyond the lens and the edit suite, I am a passionate gamer. Engaging with titles like Overwatch and Fortnite keeps my creative perspective fresh and serves as a vital way to recharge.

My professional goal is to contribute to the Dubai Government, specifically within the Dubai Media Office. I look forward to absorbing every possible skill from this course to prepare for a career in professional news reporting and content documentation.
